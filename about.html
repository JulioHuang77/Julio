About Me

I am a Taipei born designer.

Before graduated from National Taiwan University of Science and Technology, where I developed my own style and strong passion for communication design, I've already worked as internship in a domestic creative company and an American startup company of food delivery service.

As a designer specializing on graphic, branding, UX and UI, I aim to communicate strong ideas with well-visualized images and better user experiences while maintaining style consistency and intuitiveness between people and design. Besides, I also expand on my professional field to illustration and package design.

Beyond my passion for design, I truly enjoy the relationships that have grown from working with nice people and the opportunity to bring my best to further their success.

I've been focusing on solving the problems with effective solutions and clear design.
