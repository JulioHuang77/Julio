About

Hi, I'm Julio Huang

A product designer with 5+ years experience.

Specializing in user research, interaction, prototyping and UI design.

My expertise lies in transforming complex research findings into intuitive and engaging experiences for business and customers.

I have participated the growth of inline, a F&B SAAS startup, from a scale of less than 500 active users in 2018 to the leading market with 4k+ restaurants and 100k+ active users engaged among East Asia and Oceania in a rapid speed. As a product designer lead, I've built up the strongest end-to-end key product features from scratch, including table management system, queuing system, online food ordering for takeout/delivery and 10+ more.

I love going to where people will end up using the product or service I’m working on. Observing people in their environment, asking clarifying questions, validating assumptions, getting quantitative/qualitative feedback and testing prototypes to improve the overall design of the final product.

Besides...

I love drawing and sketching in my spare time. I am into the texture of watercolor paper, and enjoy controling the spread of my wash to create textures and shape them into forms. Also, I love to explore new experiences around the world. I'm proactively learning and seeking challenges to push myself forward.
